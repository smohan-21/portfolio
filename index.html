Data Analyst

Turning data into actionable business insights through analytics, machine learning, and visualization.

I bridge the gap between raw data and business decisions by transforming complex datasets into actionable insights. My approach combines analytical thinking with business context — focusing not just on what the data shows, but what decisions it can support.

I have hands-on experience across the end-to-end analytics workflow, including data cleaning, exploratory analysis, predictive modeling, visualization, and dashboard development. Every project I build is centered around a key business question and measurable outcomes.
